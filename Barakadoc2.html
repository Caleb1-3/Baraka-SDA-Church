About Us

Welcome to Baraka Seventh-day Adventist Church. We are a Bible-believing church committed to spreading the message of hope, faith, and love in Christ. Our mission is to uplift spiritual growth and strengthen fellowship among members and the community.

Our Pastor

Pastor Cyprian Odhiambo is our dedicated spiritual leader. He is passionate about guiding the congregation in faith, nurturing young believers, and leading the church in community outreach.
